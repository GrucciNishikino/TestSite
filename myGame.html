Modes of Play

Story

You just robbed a bank. The cops are on your tail though. You need to get away. Use WASD to control the burglar, and get away! Be careful, though! If the cops catch you, you get thrown in jail with a loan shark of yours!

The only way to get away is to steal a nearby car that is driving away. Afterwards, you commit other crimes, and get chased down again, this time with more cops, or faster cops!

Cop Descriptions:

Speedy:

Fastest, directly chases you.


Dimmy:

Has absolutely no clue what he is doing.


Slowclimb:

A slower version of speedy, except he goes uproad very slowly.


Gummy:

Moves pseudo-relative to you.


Darko:

His speed is directly related to his position. The chief of police.


Tank:

Starts off slow, but gets much faster as time goes on.


Warper:

Warps around to try and catch you. Usually fails, but tries anyways. Wears a purple warp cap.


Jack of All Trades:

Randomly behaves like one of the others, while changing every 3-5 seconds.


The Getaway:

Not actually a cop, but a bystander. He is stuck in the middle of this, so you must get to his car to get away!

Gamemodes:

Babby Game

Why you play babby game? Painfully easy to play. Doesn't contain Jack or Warper, and The Getaway is easy to catch. Simple bank robbery.

Easy

Not too easy, but still easy. Doesn't contain Jack or Warper, and has the advanced Getaway AI, but The Getaway is slow. Another bank robbery.

Normal

Well rounded difficulty, which has the fast-ish advanced Getaway AI, but still doesn't have Jack or Warper. This time a bigger bank was robbed.

Hard

Pretty difficult. The Getaway AI is smart, and also fast, as well as having Jack and Warper. You just vandalized some things this time.

Nightmare

Extremely difficult. All enemies have increased speed, and the Getaway is very fast. I recommend that you have mastered Hard first. Now you have taken over an office and held hostages for ransom.

Endless Mode

A constant stream of enemies appear. You also get a score. Now, you've really done it. You've trapped yourself on the interstate everything started at.

Royal Flush

8 Jacks of All Trades. You've escaped the prison you were in.

H O R R I B L E

7 Tanks, except REALLY fast. As well as the Jack of All Trades. Now you're going to be up for the death penalty.

Cops n' Robbers

One player is a burglar, and one is a cop. Player 1, try to escape before the cops get you! Player 2, try to catch the burglar before they escape!

Partners in Crime

Both players are burglars, running from the cops.

Partners Endless

Both players are burglars, running from the cops, except it is endless.
